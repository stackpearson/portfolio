I grew up in (what once was) a relatively small city called Boise in the gem state of Idaho. It's been a great home base for everything I love; fishing, camping, snowboarding and a vast variety of other outdoor activities. I received my bachelors degree in biology from our local university, Boise State University, which most of you likely know for the infamous “smurf turf” that our football team prides itself in.

I’ve always been fascinated with what technology can do for us. My father was an electrical engineer for the bulk of his career and has spent the last several years managing an eCommerce platform for local business. This spiked my interest in web development.

Ever since, I’ve been exploring the ins and outs of the internet, and learning how I can make technology work for me. I enjoy creating and sharing content in my free time, and the best way I’ve found to do that is via custom web pages. I also love to build web tools to increase my efficiency both in and out of the office.
